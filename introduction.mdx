## Overview

Welcome to the Aéroclub Air France - Toulouse documentation space. You use this centralized hub to organize, manage, and share all project-related documentation, from flight logs and maintenance schedules to training materials and regulatory compliance guides. It streamlines collaboration for pilots, instructors, and ground crew, ensuring everyone accesses the latest information efficiently.

This space supports Markdown-based pages, version history, and search functionality, making it ideal for aviation clubs handling dynamic documentation needs.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes. Follow these steps to create your first documentation page.

<Steps>

<Step title="Create a New Page" icon="plus">

  Navigate to the dashboard and select "New Page". Choose a title like "Flight Procedures".

</Step>

<Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">

  Use Markdown or MDX syntax. Here's a basic example:

  ````markdown
  ## Flight Safety Checklist

  - Pre-flight inspection
  - Weather briefing
  - Emergency procedures
  ````

</Step>

<Step title="Publish and Share" icon="share-2">

  Preview changes, then publish. Share the page URL with your team via email or Slack.

</Step>

</Steps>

<Tabs>

<Tab title="Markdown Basics" icon="code">

  Start simple with standard Markdown:

  ````markdown
  # Header

  - List item
  - Another item

  `Inline code` for commands.
  ````

</Tab>

<Tab title="MDX Components" icon="layers">

  Enhance with components:

  ````jsx

  ````
