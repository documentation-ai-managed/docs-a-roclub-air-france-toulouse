## Booking a Flight Lesson

To reserve a flight lesson:

1. Log in to your member account on the club's website.
2. Select "Réservations" from the main menu.
3. Choose your preferred date and aircraft.
4. Confirm your instructor and submit the request.
5. You will receive a confirmation email once approved.

For cancellations, use the same menu to modify or cancel your booking.

If you’re a new member, contact your instructor for first-time access details.
