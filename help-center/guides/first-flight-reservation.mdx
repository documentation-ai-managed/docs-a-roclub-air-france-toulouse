## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Log in via the member portal at https://aeroclubairfrance-toulouse.fr.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to 'Réservations'">
    Find the "Réservations" tab in the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your slot">
    Select the desired aircraft, date, and time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign instructor">
    Pick your instructor or select "Affecté automatiquement".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and confirm the reservation. You’ll get an email confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
